How It Works

Simple by Design

Here's how it works (spoiler: it's dead simple):

  • choose a value within the allowed range

  • select one of two outcomes

  • press Flip and view the result immediately

That's it. No hidden menus or weird surprises.

Coin Flip Experience

Instant Feedback

The flip animation is short and readable.
The motion builds just enough anticipation, then resolves cleanly.
You'll see the result right away - no tricks, no waiting around.

Outcome Amplification

Clear Result Logic

When the prediction matches the outcome, the selected value is amplified by a fixed factor.
This factor is visible in advance, so the result is always understood before the action.
No tricks, no gotchas - just exactly what's on the tin.

History & Overview

Previous Flips

A history section displays recent outcomes in a clear list.
This allows players to:

  • review past results

  • observe sequences

  • reflect on decisions

Sure, you can check your history, but honestly? Don't go looking for some grand pattern - it's just coin flips.
